Our Story

The launch of Tropicana Brands Group represents an exciting fusion of some of the world’s most beloved and up-and-coming brands in the juice category, including Tropicana, Naked, KeVita, Izze, Dole and Copella. Our new company blends
the best of two worlds: one steeped in industry-leading expertise and
the heritage of prestige juice brands, and another rooted in a true
entrepreneurial mindset.



The company will be focused on our purpose to nourish and delight one sip at a time, while continuing to build upon the many important diversity and sustainability activities that our people and brands have historically supported.
